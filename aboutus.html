Welcome to The Greenhouse - Your Gateway to Malta's Botanical Riches!

At The Greenhouse, we invite you on a captivating journey through the diverse and exquisite flora that graces the enchanting landscapes of Malta. Nestled in the heart of the Mediterranean, Malta boasts a unique and thriving ecosystem, where a myriad of national plants tell the story of the island's natural beauty.

Our Mission

At the core of 'The Greenhouse' it is our mission to foster a connection between people and the plant kingdom of Malta. We strive to educate, inspire, and promote awareness about the importance of preserving and appreciating the country's botanical diversity. By showcasing the beauty and significance of Malta's national plants, we hope to instill a sense of pride and environmental stewardship among our visitors.
